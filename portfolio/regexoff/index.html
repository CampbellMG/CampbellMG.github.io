RegExOff

Published on May 12, 2019 by Campbell

Challenge your regular expression skills in this free puzzle game!

RegexOff is a free puzzle game where the goal is to write regular expressions that match a set of requirements created by other players!



Features:

  • Free! No Ads! No in-app purchases! No permissions!
  • Play puzzles without an account
  • Account creation and syncing across devices
  • Create puzzles and challenge others
  • Standard RegEx (Javascript) allowing simple and complex puzzles
  • Four requirement types (full match, partial match, no match and captures)

While RegExOff is aimed at those while a fundamental understanding of RegEx, it is designed to challenge you as a part of the learning process.

If you are unfamiliar with RegEx, take a look at the FAQ on a pattern for some fundamentals!
