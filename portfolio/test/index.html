My Meal Checker

My Meal Checker

Published on May 12, 2019 by Campbell

Recipes is a Trello plugin that assists with managing energy goals and grocery lists.

Features

With the recipes plugin you can:

  • Configure energy types (Calories or Kilojoules)
  • Set an energy value on a recipe card
  • Keep track of the total energy value for a list
  • Set an energy goal for a list
  • Generate a grocery list card from card checklists

Recipes is completely free and requires no authentication (except for grocery list creation)

Grocery Lists

The create grocery list button will generate a new card in the selected list. The grocery card will contain a checklist containing a combination of all items from the first checklist in each card from the list. The resulting list will sum list items if they have a matching format, i.e.:

  - 500g chicken
  - 200g chicken

Will result in

  - 700g chicken

However,

  - 500g chicken
  - 500 g chicken
  - 500g of chicken

Will result in

  - 500g chicken
  - 500 g chicken
  - 500g of chicken
